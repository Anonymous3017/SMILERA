ABOUT US

SMILERA is a volunteer-driven, non-profit society that has been working since 2021  for economic empowerment and livelihood of women and adolescent youth 

The meaning of our name is “era of smiles” – a hope towards making a positive change in the lives of all those who are a part of this initiative and are touched by it.

It comprises of the new society through distribution of high quality and skilled products and in return workers having wages and having an all round development physically and economically.

Smilera Foundation presents you all hand- skilled products. Our aim is to introduce handicrafts in Global Market to improve economy and promote our colourful Indian culture . It will also help our Artisans and their family on a growth scale effectively.

The organization operates a livelihood where people irrespective of caste, religion, colour, and gender come together to self-manage and operate various initiatives,

“Stitch-a-Living -" This is Smilera’s flagship initiative, where women from village or undeveloped areas come together to make hand-stitched items, like handicrafts, lifestyle décor products and other merchandise. These products are designed using various materials such as kasab, tiki, sitara, cutdana and etc which are displayed in exhibitions and conferences and even sold during display. You can take a look at everything we hand-craft with love here.

All over, Smilera helps its people in availing Government schemes in education, vocational training, technical and skilled trainings and public food distribution system. It also focuses on the inner development of its community members through workshops on meditation, street theatre, hygiene, and yoga.
